Sound

About

Each sound card has to have it's own driver to modify the cards features at a register level. It is hoped that video card manufactures will in future directly provide support by developing there own Millennium drivers for their cards. Until this is done, however, the responsibility must fall upon the Millennium development team and individual Millennium users.

The Millennium Operating System provides a generic sound card interface between the kernel and the sound driver. This interface is known as the Millennium Sound Abstraction Layer (MSAL).
